Our Services

Open Testing Service (OTS) always deliver effective and efficient services to their clients. Our core services are:

Admission Tests

OTS provide evidence-based admission tests services to support selection of students in the education sector. Our services are customized, and we usually provide services at door step including paper Marking.

Recruitment Tests

OTS assists organizations in reducing the cost of recruitment by delegating recruitment administration to external specialists and making the whole process more controllable and accountable.

Enterprise Solutions

OTS Enterprise Solutions works as technical solution provider at the enterprise level. OTSES works in automation of different industry tools like data management, data screening.

Training

Our Innovative Training Products improves trainee overall skills and build capacity to handle new challenges and opportunities for lifelong learning in a competitive environment.

Research

Our goal is to make fundamental contributions to the progress of education in Pakistan through educational research and analysis, fair and valid assessments, innovative product development and informative policy studies.

Consulting

OTS offers consultancy services for Recruitment, Research, Admission Tests, Training and Development, Enterprise software solution for data management.

Employment Services

OTS provides Employment Services to public sector, private sector and individuals, the service includes full time, part time and temporary staffing.

Printing Services
